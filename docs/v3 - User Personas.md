# Version 3: User Personas
## 1. Schedule guy
My name is Pecco, I am a person who is always on time to the track and expect everyone else invovled to do the same if they are with me.
When i open this app, I want to see the schedule of the MotoGP weekend with local and remote times for me to prepare for the live race and get everything sorted before it begins.
I use this app fo just glancing on the data that i need very quickly and I mostly do this when working on something else so on my work computer mostly.
If the timings are reliable and accurate in every championship weekend during sessions it would be very great and i would recommend it to my friends too.
If i am seeing wrong timings, misplaced timings or cluttered UI, I am definitely not using this application.

## 2. Results guy
My name is Martinator, I am person of Results, I always work for the outcome and don't care how the oucome is achieved.
The most important thing I would want from an application like this is the accurate championship standings and detailed results of all categories and classes.
I mostly use my mobile phone for seeing the results when i am in my free time.
Martinator wants the results to feel alive and unambiguous — not sterile., but what makes me trust it would be consistently true results and a place where i can see penalties on riders, teams and constructors.
If the application shows inaccurate results and maye messes up the points scored by the candidates would immediately make me quit this application.
