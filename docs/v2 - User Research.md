## Summary:
This MotoGP app shows the schedule of the MotoGP weekend. The schedule contains the timing of the session in both locations (remote and original). The app also shows the championship standings. The standings include all classes and all categories. The home page also has a brief page of results/standings. The rider data page is a fun page where fans can visit to gain insights about their favourite rider.

My users are results checkers, spoiler avoiders and casual fans/newcomers. Spoiler avoiders need to see accurate timings in their own location and be able to follow the live and not miss out on them. These kind of audience mostly will be desktop first, checking results during work or during theor entertainment watching time.Results checkers view the results after each session and some view only after each race. These kkind of viewers will use mostly mobile. Casuals/fans can use the above features to see where their favourite rider is and also can explore more about their favourite riders in the rider profile page. I learned i am building a schedule first app with seconding championship standings.
